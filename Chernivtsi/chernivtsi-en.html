Chernivtsi Educational and Scientific
Law Institute

Chernivtsi Educational and Scientific Law Institute is one of the leading educational centers in Western Ukraine in the field of law. It provides training for highly qualified specialists who work in the judiciary, prosecution, advocacy, law enforcement agencies, and other legal institutions.

Founded in 2001 as an educational and consulting center, the institute gained the status of a full-fledged educational and scientific institution in 2014. Today, it offers bachelor's and master's programs in several specializations, focusing on both theoretical and practical training for students.

The institute is equipped with a modern material and technical base, including multimedia classrooms, specialized laboratories, and a library. The educational process is structured to ensure that students acquire not only profound knowledge but also real-world experience through cooperation with legal institutions.

Institute History

Chernivtsi Educational and Scientific Law Institute was established in 2001 as an educational and consulting center of the Odesa National Law Academy. In 2014, the institution gained the status of an institute, expanding its range of educational programs and strengthening its scientific potential.

The institute trains specialists capable of effectively working in various fields of law enforcement, contributing to the development and improvement of Ukraine's legal system.

Educational Opportunities

The educational process is based on four departments that provide instruction in key legal disciplines. The academic programs include the following specializations:

• Prosecution and Investigation
• Criminal Justice
• Civil and Commercial Justice
• Judicial and Administrative Activities

Students acquire not only fundamental theoretical knowledge but also practical skills through cooperation with courts, prosecutorial offices, law firms, and notary bureaus.

Scientific Activities

The institute actively advances legal science. Faculty members and students participate in research, publish articles and monographs. A significant achievement is the institute's own scientific journal, "The Bulletin of the Chernivtsi Law Institute", which serves as a platform for discussing current legal issues.

Material and Technical Resources

The institute boasts a strong material and technical base, providing comfortable learning conditions:

• Multimedia lecture halls
• Specialized laboratories
• A library with electronic resources
• Computer labs with access to legal databases

These resources contribute to an effective educational process and the training of highly qualified legal professionals.

Student Life and Career Prospects

Students of the institute participate in professional forums, scientific conferences, and legal clinics, allowing them to gain practical experience while still studying. The institute closely collaborates with government institutions and private law firms, ensuring high competitiveness of its graduates in the job market.

This academic year, 211 students were admitted, bringing the total student body to 418. The Chernivtsi Educational and Scientific Law Institute is a reliable choice for those seeking a high-quality legal education and a successful professional career!
