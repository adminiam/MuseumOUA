International Academic School Odessa

    Opened its doors in 2020 to the best students and teachers in Ukraine. The School of the Future has everything needed to prepare talented boys and girls for adult life. In a relatively short period, I.A.S.O. has become a place where winners of regional and national Olympiads in all disciplines are nurtured. Bright and comfortable classrooms, high-quality and convenient sports infrastructure (two swimming pools, an indoor sports hall, and an open-air stadium), a modern cinema and concert hall, as well as a café-dining hall that always serves delicious and healthy meals. Even after school hours, children want to stay at the institution with their peers.

    From the beginning of construction to the commissioning of the facility, nine years passed. The educational project had many skeptics and outright opponents. However, a team of like-minded individuals, led by the idea's author – Serhii Kivalov, brought the project to completion. I.A.S.O. became the first school in Odessa built from scratch in the 21st century. It continues to lead in all aspects—quality of education, learning conditions, and the level of both teachers and students. The School of the Future always looks ahead and continues to implement dozens of other projects: underground learning classrooms in shelters, comfortable student relaxation rooms, a virtual museum, and many more. Welcome to the International Academic School Odessa!
