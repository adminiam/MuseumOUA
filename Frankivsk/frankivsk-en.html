Ivano-Frankivsk Educational and Scientific Law Institute

The Ivano-Frankivsk Educational and Scientific Law Institute is a leading educational institution that trains highly qualified specialists in the field of law. The institute combines years of experience, modern teaching methodologies, and a strong material and technical base, ensuring high-quality legal education.

The institute began its operations in 2008 and has since evolved from an educational consulting center into a full-fledged educational and scientific institution. It is housed in an academic building covering over 4,000 square meters, featuring modern lecture halls, specialized laboratories, and a library. The institute provides comfortable conditions for learning and research, as well as opportunities for an active student life.

The Ivano-Frankivsk Educational and Scientific Law Institute was founded in 2008 as an educational consulting center of the Odesa National Law Academy. In 2010, it was reorganized into a faculty and later became the Prykarpattia Law Institute. The institute received its current name in 2022.

One of the key indicators of the institute's academic, scientific, and community engagement success is the employment rate of its graduates. A combination of high-quality theoretical, practical, and personal training, along with a prestigious diploma, gives young specialists a competitive edge in the job market.

Graduates of the institute work in courts, the Security Service of Ukraine, the prosecutor’s office, the justice system, the national police, and other law enforcement agencies, as well as in government bodies and local self-government institutions. Many also contribute to human rights organizations or pursue careers in advocacy and notarial practice.

Student Life

The institute's academic building has its own designated territory and includes:
• 3 lecture halls accommodating 140–150 students each;
• 3 lecture halls for 120–130 students each;
• 1 lecture hall for 80 students;
• 25 classrooms for seminars and practical sessions.

Additionally, the institute features a forensic laboratory, a computer lab, and a specialized library with a reading room. For students' physical development, there is a stadium and a sports ground, while a cafeteria and cafés provide comfortable spaces for relaxation.

Students of the institute actively participate in scientific conferences, cultural events, and sports competitions. Creative clubs and a vocal ensemble are available, and intellectual tournaments are regularly held. The institute's football team, "Jurist", represents the institution in city and regional championships.

Additionally, students are actively involved in charitable initiatives, support for the Armed Forces of Ukraine, and various volunteer programs.

International Recognition

In 2021, the institute was visited by the Minister of Education and Science of Ukraine, Serhiy Shkarlet, which confirmed the institution's significance at the national level. The institute continues to integrate into the international educational space and improve the quality of legal education.

The Ivano-Frankivsk Educational and Scientific Law Institute is a modern institution that trains highly qualified specialists for Ukraine's legal sector.
