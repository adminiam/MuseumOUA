Professional College

A Professional College is a modern educational institution that provides high-quality training for specialists in the fields of law, computer science, political science, sociology, and journalism. The program lasts three years, after which graduates receive a junior specialist qualification.

The college began operating as an independent educational institution in 2024 and currently has 715 students. The first enrollment consisted of 380 students, and with each passing year, the college continues to grow, offering new opportunities for students.

Modern classrooms are equipped with the latest multimedia technologies, ensuring a high level of the educational process. The college is part of a large educational project founded by Serhiy Kivalov and has immense potential for further development.

History and Specialties

The Professional College has a rich history that began in August 1997. By order of the Ministry of Education of Ukraine, a training complex of higher and vocational educational institutions was established at that time. It was the first educational complex of its kind in Ukraine.

The college offers programs in the following specialties:

• Law
• Computer Science
• Political Science
• Sociology
• Journalism

The educational process combines theoretical training with practical sessions, helping students acquire the necessary skills for their chosen field. The college hosts various scientific clubs, conferences, and practical seminars.

Today, the Professional College functions as a scientific, educational, methodological, and instructional institution that provides training for specialists with incomplete higher education in the field of "Law" and the qualification of a junior specialist based on general secondary education.

The college offers its students a strong academic foundation, enabling graduates to successfully establish themselves in their professional careers and continue their education to obtain a full higher education degree. The institution continuously enhances its educational process by implementing modern teaching and training methods for future legal professionals while actively integrating into the European educational space.
