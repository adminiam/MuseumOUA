National University
"Odesa Law Academy"

Over nearly three decades of continuous development, the educational institution has become a leader in legal education not only in Ukraine but throughout the Eastern European region. The University's leadership among other educational institutions is ensured by: a highly qualified faculty, modern material and technical infrastructure, progressive approaches in the educational process and science, and a developed system of cooperation with foreign universities and international institutions, as well as the university president.

     Throughout its existence, Odesa Law Academy has evolved from an autonomous law institute within the I.I. Mechnikov Odesa State University to an independent multidisciplinary higher education institution with "National" status. The pandemic, and later the war, not only did not slow down the University's development and its contribution to history being written today but, on the contrary, united the University's staff around a single goal: to remain the best, no matter what happens.
