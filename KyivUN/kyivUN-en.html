Kyiv University of Intellectual Property and Law

    Kyiv University of Intellectual Property and Law is a worthy and strong successor of the Odesa School of Law, having inherited its traditions and inexhaustible desire for continuous development. The training of highly qualified lawyers, philologists, managers, psychologists, and IT specialists is carried out according to European higher education standards. This multifunctional educational complex in the capital has become a home for ambitious, purposeful young men and women from all over Ukraine.

    Furthermore, the University serves as a regular meeting place for distinguished legal scholars and representatives of higher state authorities with teachers and students. In particular, the Presidium of the National Academy of Legal Sciences of Ukraine held meetings within KUIPL's walls with the participation of the Chairman of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine, Ruslan Stefanchuk; at the opening of the Kyiv University stadium, the Minister of Education and Science of Ukraine Serhiy Shkarlet and People's Deputy of Ukraine David Arakhamia were present, who highly praised the infrastructure and teaching quality at the higher education institution.
