The International Humanitarian University

    The International Humanitarian University (IHU) has evolved over 20 years from an initial cohort of seven students into a robust institution with a large community, setting a benchmark for non-state higher education establishments. Despite its relatively young age, IHU has gained recognition both within Ukraine and internationally. The university actively engages in career guidance activities, regularly hosting open days for prospective students and their parents. Consequently, IHU consistently achieves record enrollments of first-year students.

    The university's administration remains attuned to the demands of the labor market, continually refining its curriculum and monitoring employment trends. Students are introduced to potential employers early in their studies and undertake internships at renowned medical institutions, financial organizations, law enforcement agencies, hotels, restaurants, law firms, and linguistic bureaus. IHU operates its own medical and two dental clinics. Additionally, the university maintains an extensive exchange program, collaborating with numerous countries worldwide. No circumstance hinders the progress of this ambitious and goal-oriented institution, which the International Humanitarian University undoubtedly represents.
